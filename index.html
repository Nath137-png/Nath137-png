Our Roots

The Passion Behind the Crunch

Founded by a group of enterpreneurs in the heart of Tanzania, Peanut Power started with a simple mission: to share the most flavorful, highest-quality peanuts with the world.

Every peanut is ethically sourced from local farmers, sun-dried, and roasted in small batches to ensure that signature golden crunch. We believe in the power of community, quality, and heritage in every bite.
